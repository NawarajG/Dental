Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our dental practice

We accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, Guardian, and Blue Cross Blue Shield. We're happy to verify your benefits before your appointment and help you understand your coverage. If you don't see your insurance listed, please contact our office as we may still be able to work with your provider.

Yes, we offer flexible payment options to make dental care affordable. We accept all major credit cards, CareCredit financing, and can set up in-house payment plans for qualified patients. Our treatment coordinator will discuss all financial options with you before any procedures are performed.

For most patients, we recommend visiting every six months for a professional cleaning and check-up. However, some patients with specific conditions may need to come more frequently (every 3-4 months). Your dentist will recommend the ideal schedule based on your individual oral health needs.

If you're experiencing severe pain, swelling, bleeding, or have suffered dental trauma, please call us immediately at (123) 456-7890. We reserve time in our schedule each day for emergency patients. After hours, call the same number and follow the prompts to reach the dentist on call. For knocked-out teeth, keep the tooth moist (in milk or saliva) and try to see us within 30 minutes for the best chance of saving the tooth.
